About Me

I’m William Jiang, a software engineer from Brooklyn, NY, in the Midwood neighborhood. Before joining The Marcy Lab School, I followed the academic path I thought everyone was expected to take, going from high school to college.

Finding my major in college was tough because there weren’t many options that interested me. However, computer science sparked a fire in me. I wanted to become a software engineer. My interest came from trying to run games on computers that couldn’t handle them. I spent hours researching tutorials and tinkering with settings to improve performance. That’s when I set my goal, to become a software engineer that builds websites and applications optimized for both performance and user experience.

Although I was enthusiastic, because my high school grades were low due to lack of motivation, I attended a community college that didn’t fully have the resources to support my education. Due to my financial situation, attending other colleges was also difficult.

While looking for better opportunities, I discovered The Marcy Lab School, a one year program that gave me the training, resources, and support I needed to enter the world of software engineering.
